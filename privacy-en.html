Privacy Policy — 半句·紙上

Last updated: April 2026

半句·紙上 (hereinafter "the App") respects and protects user privacy.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

2. Voice Data

The App uses Apple's built-in speech recognition to convert voice to text. Voice data is processed in real time by Apple's system. The App does not record, store, or transmit any audio files.

3. Journal Data

Your journal entries are stored solely on your device. The App has no account system, requires no sign-up, and does not upload any content to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or push notification SDKs.

5. In-App Purchase

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase. Payment is processed through the official Apple App Store. The App does not access any payment information.
